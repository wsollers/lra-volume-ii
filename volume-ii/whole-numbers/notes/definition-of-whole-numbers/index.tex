\section{Definition of the Whole Numbers}
\label{sec:definition-of-whole-numbers}

\begin{topicbox}{The Positive Part with a New Zero}
\begin{exposition}
The whole numbers are obtained by adjoining a single new element to the
one-based natural numbers. The notation records two facts at once: the
positive natural numbers remain present by inclusion, and the new element
$0$ is not one of those positive natural numbers.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
