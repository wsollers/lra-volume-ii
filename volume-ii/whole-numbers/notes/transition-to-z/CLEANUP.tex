\section{Transition to \texorpdfstring{$\mathbb{Z}$}{Z}}
\label{sec:transition-to-z}
\begin{topicbox}{From Zero to Additive Inverses}
\begin{exposition}
The whole numbers fix the missing additive identity, but they do not make
subtraction available in general. The integer construction repairs this by
adjoining additive inverses formally.
\end{exposition}
