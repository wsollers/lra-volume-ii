\section{Extending Multiplication to \texorpdfstring{$\mathbb{W}$}{W}}
\label{sec:extending-multiplication-to-w}

\begin{topicbox}{Multiplication with Zero}
\begin{exposition}
Multiplication on $\mathbb{W}$ keeps the old natural-number multiplication
on positive inputs and adds the absorbing behavior of the new element $0$.
The cases are compatible because $0\notin\mathbb{N}$.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
