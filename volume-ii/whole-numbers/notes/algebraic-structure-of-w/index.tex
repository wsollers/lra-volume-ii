\begin{exposition}
After addition, multiplication, and order have been extended, the main point
of $\mathbb{W}$ can be recorded structurally. The whole numbers form the
zero-based arithmetic system used as the bridge from positive natural numbers
to the integer construction.
\end{exposition}
