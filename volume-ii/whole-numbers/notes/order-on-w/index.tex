\section{Order on \texorpdfstring{$\mathbb{W}$}{W}}
\label{sec:order-on-w}

\begin{topicbox}{Zero as the Least Element}
\begin{exposition}
The order on $\mathbb{W}$ extends the order on $\mathbb{N}$ and places the
new element $0$ below every whole number. Thus the positive part keeps its
old order, while the extended system gains a least element.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
