\label{sec:extending-successor}
\begin{topicbox}{Successor After Adjoining Zero}
\begin{exposition}
The new successor map on $\mathbb{W}$ agrees with the old successor on the
positive part and sends the new initial element $0$ to $1$. This makes
$\mathbb{W}$ a zero-based successor chain while preserving the one-based
successor behavior already proved on $\mathbb{N}$.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
