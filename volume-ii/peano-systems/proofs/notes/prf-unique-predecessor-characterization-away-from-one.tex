\begin{corollary*}[Unique Predecessor Characterization Away from $1$]
Let $(P,S,1)$ be a Peano system. For every $x \in P$, $x \neq 1$ if and
only if there exists a unique $u \in P$ such that $S(u)=x$.
\end{corollary*}
