\section{Extending Addition to \texorpdfstring{$\mathbb{W}$}{W}}
\label{sec:extending-addition-to-w}
\begin{topicbox}{Addition with Zero}
\begin{exposition}
Addition on $\mathbb{W}$ keeps the old natural-number addition whenever both
inputs are positive and adds identity clauses for the new element $0$. The
case split is exclusive because $0\notin\mathbb{N}$.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
