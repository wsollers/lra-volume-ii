\begin{theorem*}[Division by the Class of One]
For every $[(a,b)]\in\mathbb{Q}$,
\[
[(a,1)] = [(a,b)]\cdot[(b,1)]^{-1}
\]
and in particular $[(a,1)]$ acts as the integer $a$.  Concretely,
\[
\frac{[(a,1)]}{[(1,1)]} = [(a,1)].
\]
\end{theorem*}
