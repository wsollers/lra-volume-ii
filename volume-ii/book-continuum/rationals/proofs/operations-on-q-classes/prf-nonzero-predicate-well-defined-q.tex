For every prefraction $(a,b)\in\operatorname{Pre}\mathbb{Q}$,
\[
[a,b]\ne 0_{\mathbb{Q}}
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
a\ne 0_{\mathbb{Z}}.
\]
Thus nonzeroness is independent of the representative.
\end{lemma*}
