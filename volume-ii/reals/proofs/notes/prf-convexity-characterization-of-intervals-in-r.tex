\begin{theorem*}[Convexity Characterization of Intervals in $\mathbb{R}$]
A subset $I \subseteq \mathbb{R}$ is an interval if and only if
whenever $x,z \in I$ and $x < y < z$, then $y \in I$.
\end{theorem*}
