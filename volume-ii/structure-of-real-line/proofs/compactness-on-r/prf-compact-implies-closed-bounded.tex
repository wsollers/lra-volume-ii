\begin{remark*}[Proof structure]
Bounded: the cover $\{B_n(0)\}_{n\in\mathbb{N}}$ has a finite subcover, whose largest ball bounds $K$. Closed: for $x\notin K$, cover $K$ by complements of shrinking closed balls about $x$; a finite subcover leaves a ball about $x$ disjoint from $K$, so the complement is open.
\end{remark*}
