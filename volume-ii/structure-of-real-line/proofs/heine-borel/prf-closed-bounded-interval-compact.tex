\begin{remark*}[Proof structure]
Let $\{U_i\}$ be an open cover and $S=\{x\in[a,b]:[a,x]\text{ has a finite subcover}\}$. Then $a\in S$, $S$ is bounded above by $b$, so $c=\sup S$ exists by completeness. Some $U_i$ contains $c$ and hence an interval about $c$; this both shows $c\in S$ and, unless $c=b$, that a slightly larger point is in $S$ --- contradiction. Hence $c=b\in S$.
\end{remark*}
