\begin{remark*}[Proof structure]
Forward direction is the previous theorem. Reverse: if $K$ is closed and bounded, then $K\subseteq[a,b]$ for some interval; $[a,b]$ is compact, and a closed subset of a compact set is compact (any open cover of $K$, together with the open complement of $K$, covers $[a,b]$).
\end{remark*}
