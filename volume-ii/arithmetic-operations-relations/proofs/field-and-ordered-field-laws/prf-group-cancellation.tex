\begin{theorem*}[Group Cancellation]
In a group, cancellation holds on both sides:
\[
a\ast c=b\ast c\Longrightarrow a=b
\qquad\text{and}\qquad
c\ast a=c\ast b\Longrightarrow a=b.
\]
\end{theorem*}
