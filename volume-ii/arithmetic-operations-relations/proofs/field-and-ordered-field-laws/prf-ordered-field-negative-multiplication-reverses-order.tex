\begin{theorem*}[Ordered Field Negative Multiplication Reverses Order]
In an ordered field,
\[
c<0\land a<b
\Longrightarrow
c\cdot b<c\cdot a.
\]
The corresponding non-strict implication also holds.
\end{theorem*}
