\section{Embedding \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb W\)}{W} into \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb Z\)}{Z}}
\label{sec:embedding-w-into-z}

\begin{topicbox}{From \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb W\)}{W} to \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb Z\)}{Z}}
\begin{exposition}
The passage from whole numbers to integers adds additive inverses. The whole
numbers embed as the nonnegative part of the integer system. Under this
identification, zero remains zero, successor corresponds to adding one, and
the old addition and multiplication agree with integer addition and
multiplication on the embedded image. What changes is not the arithmetic of
the whole numbers, but the ambient system in which subtraction is now always
available.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
