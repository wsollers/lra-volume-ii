\section{Embedding \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb Q\)}{Q} into \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb R\)}{R}}
\label{sec:embedding-q-into-r}

\begin{topicbox}{From \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb Q\)}{Q} to \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb R\)}{R}}
\begin{exposition}
The real numbers are introduced to repair the completeness failures of the
rationals. The rational numbers embed into the real numbers in a way that
preserves field operations and order. After this embedding is fixed, rational
numbers can be treated as real numbers without changing their arithmetic, but
the surrounding system now contains limits, cuts, or equivalence classes that
were not present in \(\mathbb Q\).
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
