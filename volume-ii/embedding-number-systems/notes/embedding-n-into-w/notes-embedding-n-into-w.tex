\section{Embedding \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb N\)}{N} into \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb W\)}{W}}
\label{sec:embedding-n-into-w}

\begin{topicbox}{From \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb N\)}{N} to \texorpdfstring{\(\mathbb W\)}{W}}
\begin{exposition}
The first embedding records that the natural-number system sits inside the
whole-number system after the new zero element has been introduced. The
successor structure is preserved: the image of the first natural number is
the successor of zero, and applying successor before or after the embedding
gives the same result. Addition, multiplication, and order on the natural
numbers agree with the restrictions of the corresponding operations and order
on the whole numbers.
\end{exposition}
\end{topicbox}
