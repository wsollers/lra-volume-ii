\begin{theorem*}[Natural-Number Order Is Total]
Let $(P,S,1)$ be a Peano system. For every $a,b \in P$,
\[
a \le b
\qquad\text{or}\qquad
b \le a.
\]
Consequently $\le$ is a total order on $P$.
\end{theorem*}
