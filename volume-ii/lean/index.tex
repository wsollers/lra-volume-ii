\noindent\textbf{How we got here.}
The volume uses ordinary mathematical prose and also keeps an eye on formal
proof assistants. Lean 4 is introduced first as a notation bridge, not as a
replacement for the mathematical development.

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{What this chapter develops.}
This chapter gives a quick orientation to Lean 4 syntax used in the Peano
system material, especially the way predicates and subsets are represented as
functions into `Prop`.

\medskip
\noindent\textbf{Where this leads.}
The next chapter develops Peano systems in ordinary mathematical notation. The
Lean notation introduced here makes the corresponding formalization easier to
read when it appears alongside the notes.
